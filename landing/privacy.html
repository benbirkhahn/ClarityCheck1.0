1. Introduction

At ClarityCheck, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

ClarityCheck scans PDFs for hidden content, invisible text, AI trap instructions, and tracking watermarks. Our analysis requires server-side processing, but we've designed our system to minimize data exposure and maximize your privacy.

By using our service, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

✓ What We Collect

  • Email address (waitlist or account)
  • Uploaded PDFs (temporarily, for analysis)
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Basic technical info (browser, device)

✗ What We Don't Collect

  • Document content beyond analysis
  • Personal data from within your PDFs
  • Device fingerprints or cross-site tracking
  • Payment card numbers (handled by Stripe)

If you join our waitlist, we collect your email solely to notify you about product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time. If you create an account, passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

3. Document Handling

Key principle: Your uploaded documents are used exclusively for analysis and are automatically deleted after processing. We do not read, store, or share document contents beyond delivering your scan results.

During Processing

  • Files are encrypted in transit using TLS (HTTPS)
  • Temporarily stored on our servers only during analysis
  • Analyzed by our 11 detection engines for hidden content and AI traps

After Processing

  • Original uploaded files are automatically deleted
  • Cleaned PDFs available for limited-time download, then deleted
  • Only analysis metadata (risk scores, findings) may be retained for your account

What We Never Do

  • Never use documents to train AI models
  • Never share documents with third parties
  • Never sell or rent access to uploaded files
  • Never retain document content beyond the processing window

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Processing PDF uploads, delivering scan results, managing accounts
  • Service Improvement: Understanding usage patterns, fixing bugs, improving detection accuracy
  • Communication: Waitlist notifications and product updates (with your consent)

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, behavioral profiling, selling to third parties, or training AI models.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your information. We may share limited data with:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (they handle all card data directly)
  • Cloud Hosting — Server infrastructure for running our analysis
  • Analytics — Anonymous website usage statistics

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect against fraud. Even then, we share only the minimum necessary.

6. Data Security

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure cloud hosting infrastructure
  • Bcrypt password hashing (never stored in plain text)
  • JWT-based authentication with secure token handling
  • Automatic file deletion after processing
  • Regular security updates and dependency patching

Note: No system is 100% secure. We minimize risk by retaining as little data as possible for as short a time as necessary.

7. Data Retention

  • Uploaded Documents: Automatically deleted after processing completes
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active; deletable on request
  • Waitlist Emails: Retained until launch notification, unsubscribe, or deletion request
  • Analytics: Anonymous data retained per provider's standard policy (14–26 months)

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access — Request info about what data we hold
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — Request a copy of your data
  • Object — Object to processing (e.g., opt out of analytics)
  • Withdraw Consent — Stop using the service at any time

To exercise any right, email us at the address below. We respond within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — Payment processing. We never see your full card details. Stripe Privacy
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage tracking. Google Privacy

You can control third-party tracking via browser settings, ad blockers, or analytics opt-out tools.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will update the "Last updated" date and provide notice for significant changes. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
