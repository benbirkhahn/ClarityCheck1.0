About ClarityCheck

Making invisible document threats visible — so you can work with AI safely.

PDFs are everywhere — in education, business, and government. But most people don't realize their documents can contain hidden instructions that manipulate AI tools, invisible tracking watermarks, and content designed to deceive. ClarityCheck was built to solve that problem. We give you full visibility into what's really inside your documents — before you share them with AI assistants, colleagues, or the world.
