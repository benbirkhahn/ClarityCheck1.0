2. Service Description

ClarityCheck is a web-based document analysis tool that detects and removes hidden content from PDF files, including:

  • Zero-width and invisible Unicode characters
  • Color-matched (white-on-white) hidden text
  • Off-screen and tiny text positioned outside visible boundaries
  • Invisible rendering modes and opacity-hidden content
  • Hidden annotations and suspicious metadata
  • AI trap instructions and prompt injection attempts
  • Layered text, visual mismatches, and suspicious spacing

The Service requires PDF upload to our servers for processing. All uploads are encrypted in transit and files are automatically deleted after processing. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
